question=Without using explicit loop constructs or if, construct a 1,000 x 1,000 matrix containing a NE/SW diagonal up-down ramp as shown below &ndash; note every NE/SW diagonal stripe has the same value.
examples=0   1   2   &hellip; 997 998 999<br />  1   2   3   &hellip; 998 999 998<br />  2   3   4   &hellip; 999 998 997<br />  &hellip;   &hellip;   &hellip;   &hellip;   &hellip;   &hellip;   &hellip;<br />997 998 999   &hellip;   4   3   2<br />998 999 998   &hellip;   3   2   1 <br />999 998 997   &hellip;   2   1   0
