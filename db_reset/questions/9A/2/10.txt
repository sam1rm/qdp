question=Without using explicit loop constructs or if, define smooth, a new smoothed column vector by inserting new data in-between each row that is the average of the surrounding rows.
examples=examples=Before   After<br /><br />data =   smooth =<br />  1        1<br />  3        2<br /> -3        3<br /> -7        0<br />          -3<br />          -5<br />         - 7
